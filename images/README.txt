## Tips
- Get photos from your Google Maps listing (customer-uploaded images)
- Screenshot the menu photos from Google Maps for now — you can upgrade later
- If a photo is missing, the category card shows its gradient colour instead (it still looks good)
- Free stock photos: https://unsplash.com · https://pexels.com
